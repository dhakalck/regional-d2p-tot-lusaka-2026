Pre-Workshop Preparation

Required Reading Before the Workshop

To maximize your learning during the TOT workshop, please review the following materials before arrival:

  1. D2P Mentor Guide - Read in full to understand the mentorship role
  2. D2P Module Summaries - Familiarize yourself with all 20 modules
  3. 10 Steps to Developing a Policy Brief - Review the policy development process
  4. Policy Brief Checklist - Understand quality criteria for policy briefs
  5. Sample Policy Briefs - Review at least 2-3 examples

Note: These materials will be sent to you via email 2 weeks before the workshop. Please allocate 4-6 hours for pre-workshop reading.
