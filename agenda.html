March 2-5, 2026 | Ciela Lusaka, Zambia

Overview

The 4-day Training of Trainers workshop focuses on interactive learning, presentations, and mentoring practice. Each session includes opportunities for hands-on application, feedback, and preparation for real-world D2P implementation.

Daily Schedule:

  • Day 4 (March 5): 8:45 AM - 12:00 PM
